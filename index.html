Welcome to Filipino Bakery!


Welcome to Filipino Bakery, where we bring the heart of the Philippines to every bite. Founded with a passion for sharing the rich flavors and traditions of Filipino baking, we pride ourselves on offering an authentic experience that celebrates both classic and contemporary Filipino pastries. From the beloved pan de sal to the sweet biko and indulgent ensaymada, our bakery uses only the finest ingredients to create pastries that capture the warmth and hospitality of Filipino culture.
