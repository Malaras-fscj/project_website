Reviews

As a Filipino living abroad, finding a bakery that brings me back home is a dream come true! This bakery offers all my favorite pastries, from the sweet, soft pandesal to the decadent ensaymada. Their bibingka is also a must-try — it’s just like how my grandmother used to make it! The flavors are so authentic, and you can taste the love in every bite. The staff is always friendly and the bakery is always clean and welcoming. Highly recommend it to anyone craving Filipino comfort food!

Carolina Traveler

I’ve been coming here for months now and I can honestly say this is the best Filipino bakery in the area. The pandesal is perfect — light, fluffy, and just the right amount of sweetness. The leche flan is another favorite; it’s creamy and not too sweet, which is just how I like it. They also have a great selection of traditional Filipino cakes like ube and mango float. If you're new to Filipino desserts, this is the place to start!

Mikey of Jacksonville

I can’t get enough of this bakery! Every time I stop by, they have fresh, warm pandesal right out of the oven. It’s the best way to start the day. The variety of Filipino pastries they offer is amazing, from crispy lumpia to rich and velvety ube halaya. The customer service is always friendly and helpful. Whether you're looking for a snack or catering for a special event, this place never disappoints.
